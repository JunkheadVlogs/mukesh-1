Our Legacy Since 1978

Founded in the heart of Nagpur's historic textile market, Mukesh Saree Centre has been the premier destination for premium silk sarees, bridal trousseaus, and custom handloom drapes for over four decades. We bring together time-tested weaver traditions with premium fabrics like Banarasi Georgette, pure Organza silks, high-density Linens, and premium co-ord sets.
